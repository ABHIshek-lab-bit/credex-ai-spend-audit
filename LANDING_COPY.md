## FAQ

### 1. Is this really free?
Yes. No credit card, no login, no catch. We built this to help startups optimize their AI spend. If you're spending a lot, we'll introduce you to Credex for discounted credits—but the audit is free regardless.

### 2. How accurate are the recommendations?
Every recommendation is based on official vendor pricing (cited in our PRICING_DATA.md) and your specific use case. We don't manufacture savings—if your spend is optimized, we'll tell you.

### 3. Do I need to sign up?
Nope. You can complete the audit and see results without any login. We only ask for your email if you want to save your report or talk to Credex about bigger savings.

### 4. What tools do you support?
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Google Gemini, and Windsurf. We're adding more based on user requests.

### 5. How do you make money?
We're built by Credex, which sells discounted AI credits. If your audit shows big savings opportunities (>$500/month), we'll offer to connect you with Credex. But the audit is free and useful even if you never talk to us.
